Welcome to St Josephs Greater Noida

St. Joseph's School is an un-aided Christian minority institution owned by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Agra Education Society, Wazirpura Road, Agra-282 003, (U.P.) with the motto : “To work and serve in Love”, and is open to children of all communities. The society was allotted a plot of land by the Greater Noida Development Authority in 1993.
The construction of the presently completed 1st phase of the building began in May 1995 and completed in March 2000. The millennium project of the school took off in style on 19th March-2000, the Patrons Day of the school. On the 8th of April 2000, the academic session was inaugurated by the honourable District Magistrate, Mr. Deepak Kumar IAS.
